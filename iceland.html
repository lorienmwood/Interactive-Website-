    • ICELAND RING ROAD ITINERARY - 7-DAY ROAD TRIP + MAPS

    Embark on an unforgettable road trip around Iceland's Ring Road, a journey that promises breathtaking landscapes, cascading waterfalls, and otherworldly experiences. Here's a detailed 7-day itinerary to help you make the most of your adventure.

    Day 1: Reykjavik to Golden Circle

    Start your journey in Reykjavik and head to the iconic Golden Circle. Explore the historic Thingvellir National Park and the Silfra Fissure, witness erupting geysers at the Geysir Geothermal Area, and marvel at the powerful two-tiered Gullfoss Waterfall. Overnight in the Golden Circle area.

    Day 2: South Coast Wonders

    Embark on a picturesque drive along the South Coast. Visit the majestic Seljalandsfoss Waterfall, where you can walk behind the cascade. Admire the 60-meter high Skogafoss Waterfall and explore the unique black sand beach and basalt columns at Reynisfjara. Spend the night in the charming village of Vik.

    Day 3: Vatnajokull National Park

    Discover the natural wonders of Vatnajokull National Park. Hike along the impressive Fjaðrárgljúfur Canyon, explore the trails and Svartifoss waterfall in Skaftafell, and witness floating icebergs at the Jokulsarlon Glacier Lagoon. Stroll along Diamond Beach with ice chunks. Overnight in the Hofn area.

    Day 4: East Fjords

    Embark on a scenic drive along the East Fjords, passing through picturesque fishing villages. Explore Petra's Stone Collection in Stodvarfjordur, visit the colorful village of Seydisfjordur, and spend the night in the East Fjords.

    Day 5: North Iceland

    Experience the power of Dettifoss, Europe's most powerful waterfall. Explore the horseshoe-shaped Asbyrgi Canyon and consider a whale watching tour in Husavik, the whale-watching capital. Overnight in the Akureyri area.

    Day 6: Akureyri to West Iceland

    Explore Akureyri, the northern capital, and visit Godafoss, the "Waterfall of the Gods." See the iconic Hvitserkur rock formation and explore the Settlement Center in Borgarnes. Spend the night in West Iceland.

    Day 7: West Iceland to Reykjavik

    Visit Deildartunguhver, Europe's most powerful hot spring, and witness the unique lava waterfalls of Hraunfossar and Barnafoss. Conclude your trip with a visit to the UNESCO World Heritage Site, Thingvellir National Park, before returning to Reykjavik.
